Grimsythe Fortress

You have entered the Grimsythe Fortress...

The repugnent smell of blood and mold fills the air. You feel a chill run down your spine and get that feeling that something... somewhere... is watching you...

You grip your weapon tightly. Sounds of howling wind creep through broken winds and dark corridors

Moving forward, you now enter into what looks like a dining hall. The table is set with plates, silverware and cups. The spread over the table however; is rotten. From the looks of it, the count of this fortress and his mean were invaded before they could have their supper.

You begin to wonder to yourself. What could of happened to the count and his men? What foul creature or evil force now haunts this once noble fortess?

You hear a loud crash coming from the Count's main chamber! You loud screams of some foreign beast echo through the dining hall!! You get the feeling you are about to get the answer to your questions very soon. You gather up your courage and prepare yourself to investigate.
